Behind the Scenes at Café Gusto

Behind the vibrant hustle and bustle of the Sugarland campus at the University of Houston, lies Café Gusto, a hidden gem that has become a culinary haven for not just the students, but also for the wider community with a taste for Turkish and Italian cuisine. Each morning before sunrise, Jefy and her team of chefs are already bustling in the kitchen, preparing ingredients that will transform into exquisite dishes and aromatic coffees. At the heart of Café Gusto's operation is a spirit of teamwork and mutual support. Each staff member, while specialized in their tasks, is ready to assist others, ensuring the café's smooth operation even during peak hours. The method of collaboration extends to their service, where quality food and customer satisfaction are paramount. Jefy believes the true measure of their success isn’t just in the delicacies they serve but in the joy and satisfaction reflected in their customers' faces.

Meet Cafe Gusto's team of Chefs. Each Chef has great experience with cuisines from Italy and Turkey and is dedicated to using their culinary expertise to create appetizing dishes for diners to enjoy.

Our team of chefs working on creating new menu dishes with flavors from Italy and Turkey.
